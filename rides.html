Our Rides

Take in the sights! Enjoy a view of the expansive horizons, 150 feet in the air! Whether you're looking for the perfect moment to capture that romantic sunset, or you're just looking for a serene escape above the clouds, the Ferris Wheel is a ride you don't want to skip out on!
No need to be fearful of Whiplash, our bumper cars are both exciting and safe for the whole family! Visit this attraction for non-stop thrills and enjoyment, as you get to butt-heads with friends and strangers alike in this free-for-all battle royale. Get behind the wheel and prove your skills, or strap in with a buddy to just enjoy the ride, how you want to experience this thrill-ride of an attraction is up to you!
Enter a chamber where the laws of physics do not apply. You will defy the restrictions imposed by space-time as you warp beyond the event horizon! Be warned the journey is not for the faint of heart! The wormhole will vortex you into another dimension, and making it out to the other side will automatically grant you the title of an official Astroleaper! If vertigo and nausea are some of your weakness, you need not apply for this expedition, but for anyone brave enough to heed the call, I say to you, "Godspeed!"
Thrill-seekers, lend me your ears! Frisbee is one of our most prolific, high intensity ride for all park-goers. Twirled and swung 40 feet into the air, good luck catching your breath. Stomach drops and pre-emptive tummy butterflies should be an indication that the Frisbee is the perfect destination for adventure-seeking individuals, but be careful - make sure to keep your lunch inside your stomach after getting off this dizzying attraction!
A time-less classic, the whimsical Carousel is an essential tradition in every amusement park across the country. Mount a horse, a chariot or even a wagon as you coast along to the bohemian tunes of yesteryear. Fun for all ages, this safe and idyllic attraction cannot be missed!
Inside a chamber and being spun 12 ways round, the zipper is a ride for the truly daring. An almost macabre take on the fidelity of a traditional ferris wheel, the zipper is more akin to the frightening - yet exhilirating - rush of bungie jumping! A ride suited for only the most bold and daring thrill-seekers, if you're looking for an adrenaline rush, the Zipper will not disappoint!
Shrink down to a size no binger than an ordinary mouse, as you and your friends or family take a swirl in one of our many tea cups. Spin round and round and your tea cup swirls across the platform. A whimsical and magical time to be had is just waiting for you to grab a cup of tea and take a seat!
Soar high above the clouds as the wave swinger spins you round and round. Grab a swing with a friend and share this aerial experience with them as you streak the skys with your screams of delight, but remember - tie those shoes tight and hold em close!
